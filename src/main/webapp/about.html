Forward is a
Journey.

It’s a journey for job seekers on a quest to find a place where they
belong.

Its a journey for businesses searching for a new addition to their
ever-growing family.

And it’s a journey for us, who are trying to create a world where
these two can find each other, as fairly and stress-free as possible.

Our Story

Our journey began with a deep-seated frustration over the
inadequacy of existing job hunting resources and a lack of clarity
regarding our position in the job market.

The current hiring landscape suffers from a shortage of skilled
talent, an number of applicants, and a time-
consuming recruitment process.

We recognized the gap between the needs of companies and the
resources available to job seekers. We set out to bridge this gap by
providing a platform that offers comprehensive support to job
seekers while also meeting the needs of companies and
recruiters.
